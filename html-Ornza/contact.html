ORNZA specializes in high-fidelity luxury artificial jewellery. Our base metals are either 925 Sterling Silver, hypoallergenic lead-free brass, or anti-tarnish alloys. They are electrochemically layered with 5 coatings of 18k or 22k gold vermeil. For stones, we use hand-set conflict-free laboratory moissanites (VVS1 grade) and high-refraction Swiss cubic zirconia that capture the exact refractive presence of diamonds.
