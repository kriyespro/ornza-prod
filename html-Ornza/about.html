The Legend of ORNZA

Story & Heritage

Bridging the gap between the staggering cost of gold and the desire for high-fashion, uncompromising luxury.

Established in 2025 in Surat, India—the absolute heart of the global diamond cutting and trade industry—ORNZA was born with a singular, clear vision: to democratize high-luxury jewelry.

For generations, the weight and allure of gold and diamonds represented ultimate grace, but remained locked behind steep pricing. We believed that elegance should be an everyday experience, not an occasional compromise.

By combining Surat's century-old stone-setting heritage with state-of-the-art metallurgical plating, we created a new standard in artificial jewellery. Our pieces carry the exact density, micro-engraving, and refractive index of fine heirloom gold, at a fraction of the cost.

Surat Karigars

Our designs are hand-cast and set by local master artisans whose families have set gold and diamonds for generations.

Ethical Sourcing

We use only conflict-free laboratory moissanites, Swiss-cut cubic zirconia, and recycled sterling silver base alloys.

Anti-Tarnish Plating

Layered in thick 18k and 22k gold vermeil, finished with a vacuum-sealed coating to guard against water and sweat.

The Crafting Process

Each ORNZA piece goes through a 9-step production lifecycle: starting with 3D CAD modeling, moving to traditional hand-carved wax masters, high-density metal casting, mirror polishing, electrochemical plating (5 layers of 18k or 22k gold), microscopic prong-setting of individual stones, and lastly, dual-stage protective curing.
